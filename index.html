Manifest brings every part of the skydiving experience into one beautifully designed app.

Jump Logbook

Log every skydive with exit altitude, freefall time, canopy type, DZ location, weather conditions, formation details, and personal notes. Your complete jump history — always in your pocket, beautifully organised, and exportable anytime.

Core Feature

Gear Tracker

Track container, reserve, AAD, helmet, and all components. Full service history. Know exactly what's on your back before every jump.

OCR Data Card Scan

Point your camera at any gear data card and Manifest reads it instantly. No more manual entry — just scan and go.

Expiry Reminders

Smart alerts for reserve repacks, AAD service windows, and any gear deadline — well before they're due. Stay compliant, stay safe.

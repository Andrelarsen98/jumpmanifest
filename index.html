Why Manifest

Proof, not trust-me-bro

Skydiving still runs on paper logbooks and word of mouth. Manifest turns your jumps, your currency and your gear's service history into one verified record — witnessed, signed and time-stamped — so it holds up at the dropzone, every time.

How it works

One record that proves it all

Every jump and every service feeds a single verified history — one that follows you to the dropzone and keeps your gear in date.

1

Log every jump

In seconds — date, aircraft, altitude and discipline, auto-counted with your currency and milestones.

2

Verify & check in

Friends sign off the jumps they witnessed by scanning your QR. Show your Check-in Pass at the dropzone to prove licence and currency — and get on the load faster.

3

Keep your kit in date

Your reserve repack and AAD service live in your Gear Locker — rigger-signed and always current, so you and any DZ can trust your rig is in date.
